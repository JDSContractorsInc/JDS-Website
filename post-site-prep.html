Site Prep 101: Costs & Timeline

What to know before you break ground.

Overview

Before framing begins, proper sitework protects budget and schedule. This includes clearing/grading, erosion control, utilities, and temporary power.

Typical Items

  • Clearing and grading
  • Erosion control
  • Utilities & connections
  • Temporary power/water

Timeline

Most sites are prepped in 1–3 weeks depending on jurisdiction and weather.
