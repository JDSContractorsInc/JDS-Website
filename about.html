Who We Are

JDS Contractors Inc. is a minority family-owned residential contractor serving Maryland, Virginia, Washington, DC, Delaware, and West Virginia. We partner with general contractors and homeowners to deliver new construction, additions, and remodeling.

We specialize in rough framing, exterior enclosure (siding, trim, roofing, windows, decks), and interiors. Our crews bring discipline, safety, and craftsmanship to every project.

  • 500+ new homes framed
  • 250+ additions completed
  • 100+ decks built
  • MHIC License #113205

Mission: Build with craftsmanship, transparency, and respect.
Vision: Be the DMV's most trusted residential construction partner.
