About JDS

JDS Contractors Inc. is a minority family-owned residential contractor serving Maryland, Virginia, Washington, DC, Delaware, and West Virginia. We partner with general contractors and homeowners to deliver new construction, reconstruction, and remodeling.

We specialize in rough framing, exterior enclosure (siding, trim, roofing, windows, decks), and interiors (drywall and paint). We’ve contributed to the full new construction of 500+ single-family homes, completed 250+ additions, and built 100+ decks.

Mission: Build with craftsmanship, transparency, and respect for our clients and crews.
Vision: To be the DMV’s most trusted partner for residential construction—versatile, reliable, and fair-priced.
