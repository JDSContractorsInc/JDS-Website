How It Works

Our Process

Building should feel exciting — not overwhelming. Here's exactly how we work from first contact to final handover.

Step by Step

A Clear Path from
Start to Finish

01

Submit Your Scope

Share your drawings, architectural plans, or project description through our quote request form. No project is too large or too early-stage — we work with clients from concept through completion.

  • Upload drawings or plans
  • Describe your vision and timeline
  • Tell us your target budget range
02

Fixed-Price Bid

We review your scope and deliver a clear, itemized bid — typically within 48 hours. No vague estimates, no hidden line items. You'll know exactly what you're paying before a single nail is driven.

  • Itemized cost breakdown
  • Material and labor separated
  • Milestone payment schedule
03

Permits & Planning

Once you approve the bid, we handle all permitting, scheduling, and pre-construction logistics. You don't have to chase the county — we do it for you.

  • Permit applications & submissions
  • Subcontractor coordination
  • Material procurement & scheduling
04

Build Phase

Construction begins with regular milestone updates sent directly to you. Our project manager is your single point of contact — available by phone Mon–Sat, 8am–5pm.

  • Weekly progress updates
  • Milestone photos & reports
  • On-site supervision throughout
05

Final Walkthrough

Before we consider a project closed, you walk the entire space with our project manager. Any item you flag gets addressed before sign-off. We don't close until it's right.

  • Full site walkthrough with PM
  • Punch list resolution
  • Final inspection & sign-off
Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How long does a quote take? +
For most residential scopes, we turn around a fixed-price bid within 48 hours of receiving complete plans or a detailed project description.
Do you handle permits? +
Yes — we manage the full permitting process including applications, submissions, and inspections. You don't need to deal with the county.
What areas do you serve? +
We serve the full DMV — Maryland (all counties), Washington DC, and Northern Virginia. We also take select projects in Delaware, Pennsylvania, and West Virginia.
Are you licensed and insured? +
Yes. JDS Contractors Inc. holds MHIC License #113205 and carries full general liability and workers' compensation insurance.
What's included in a fixed-price bid? +
Our bids include a complete itemized breakdown of labor, materials, and subcontractor costs with a milestone-based payment schedule. Scope changes require a written change order with updated pricing.
