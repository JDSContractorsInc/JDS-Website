Composite vs. Wood Decks

Durability, maintenance, and cost—made simple.

Pros & Cons

Composite offers lower maintenance and longer warranties; wood offers lower upfront cost and a classic look.

Budget Tips

  • Consider lifetime cost, not just materials.
  • Rails and lighting affect price more than you expect.
  • Flashing and waterproofing matter for longevity.
